Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Capsule Corp Indonesia ("we", "us", or "our") built the Solitaire app ("the App") as a free, ad-supported application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for your name, email, or any other personally identifiable information.

All game data (scores, statistics, settings) is stored locally on your device and is never sent to any server.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (based on IP)
  • App usage data

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings. You may also purchase the ad-free option within the App to remove all advertisements entirely.

For more information on how Google handles data, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional one-time purchase to remove advertisements. All transactions are processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no data to retain. Your game data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the App.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob — for serving advertisements
  • Apple App Store — for in-app purchases (iOS)
  • Google Play Store — for in-app purchases (Android)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
